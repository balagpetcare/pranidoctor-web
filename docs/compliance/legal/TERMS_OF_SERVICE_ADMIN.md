# Admin Acceptable Use Policy (v2026-06-01)

**Document key:** `TOS-ADMIN`  
**Audience:** `ADMIN`, `SUPER_ADMIN`, `SUPPORT`

## Personnel obligations

- Protect customer and provider data; access only as required for your role
- Do not export bulk data without authorization
- Follow provider verification and content moderation procedures

## Prohibited conduct

- Sharing admin credentials
- Bypassing audit trails or moderation workflows
- Using admin tools for personal or unauthorized purposes
